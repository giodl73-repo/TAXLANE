# Wave: Tax Civics Foundation

## Goal

Create the INCOME-TAX repo foundation and define the first research path for
explaining the income tax, its history, its current mechanics, and the
program-linked tax reform hypothesis.

## Thesis

Income taxation is easier to debate honestly when the public can distinguish the
legal tax instrument, the revenue stream, the spending authority, and the public
programs being funded. The foundation wave establishes that separation before
choosing a reform design.
