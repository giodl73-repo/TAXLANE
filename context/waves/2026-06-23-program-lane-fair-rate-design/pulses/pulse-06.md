## Goal

Bring multiple points of view to the question "what is the right rate to collect"
under the hard balanced-budget rule (spend = collect, no borrowing), using the
repo's eight `.roles` review lenses.

## Changes

- Added `reviews/2026-06-23-fair-rate-panel.md`:

    decision label.
  - Convergences: two-sided gap; US is low-tax (room to collect); health is the
