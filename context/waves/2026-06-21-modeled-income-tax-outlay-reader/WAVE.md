# Wave: Modeled Income-Tax Outlay Reader

## Goal

Create a public reading packet that explains the modeled income-tax outlay
allocation work in plain language.

## Thesis

The data work is now strong enough to support a careful reader-facing page. The
page must keep three ideas separate: ordinary individual income tax is a general
receipt under the current model, the allocation view is an educational
proportional model, and deficit context is part of the story.
