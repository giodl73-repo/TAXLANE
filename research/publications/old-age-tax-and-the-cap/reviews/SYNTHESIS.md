## P1s for round 2

1. **Gate fail — the contribution-rate column** (PF-2). Uncited (mis-attributed to the
   pension-spend source), mixes single-pillar/multi-pillar/capped systems without
   labels, and "below peers" is contradicted by capped Canada (11.9% < 12.4%). Fix:
   source + year-stamp + pillar/cap-label each rate (add a peer-rate source), or drop
   the column and discuss rates qualitatively; reconcile the "below peers" framing.
2. **Horizon mixing** (PF-1, PF-4, PF-6). The "20%/70% of the 75-year shortfall" are
   CBO/SSA *actuarial-balance* scores — present them as such, not as 10-year dollars
   divided by the 75-year payroll-% deficit; caveat the two denominators.
3. **Distribution depth** (PF-5). Add an incidence subsection: **lifetime** (not annual)
   incidence, **differential mortality by income** (which offsets formula
   progressivity), and the benefit formula's own progressivity.
