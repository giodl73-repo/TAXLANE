# Revision Plan — The American Health Funding Premium (after Round 1)

Round 1 avg 2.43/4; gate not cleared (PF-7 sourcing fail). Items ordered by
severity; gate-blockers first.

## P1 — gate-blocking (must close for Round 2)

1. **Reframe the efficiency savings as conditional ceilings, not bankable dollars.**
   (PF-1, PF-3, PF-4) Remove "closes 11–33% of the deficit" from the abstract and
   conclusion. Present $198B/$395B/$593B as *potential ceilings conditional on a
   verified, coverage-protected unit-price reduction*, and state plainly that the
   delivery mechanism is out of scope, so the paper does not claim the savings are
   achievable by the funding lever alone.
2. **Register the derived records in the source ledger.** (PF-7) Add ledger entries
   (ID, derivation/provenance, path, observed date) for the `income_tax_outlay_model`
   decade-summary and `health_efficiency_scenarios` records, or remove the tables.
   Disambiguate HI receipts ($395,350M) from the 20% efficiency figure ($395,046M).

## P2 — should-fix

3. **Add an outcomes/coverage measure** (PF-3) — at least the US uninsured rate and
   one outcome metric, so "narrower coverage for twice the cost" is shown, not
   asserted; cite a price-vs-volume source for "price not utilization."
4. **Add a multi-year trajectory + the aging driver** (PF-4) — note that demographic
   enrollment growth, not only unit cost, drives the long-run path; show or cite a
   forward trajectory; clarify that an efficiency gain is a level shift, not a
   growth-rate bend.
5. **Add a distributional/incidence subsection** (PF-5) — burden by income (HI
   payroll vs general revenue vs compulsory private), the provider-incidence of the
   squeeze (safety-net/rural/Medicaid exposure), and the Medicare-vs-Medicaid
   beneficiary split.
6. **Have the abstract inherit the §8 fungibility caveat** (PF-6); round all dollar
   figures ($198B/$395B/$593B), drop the $197,523M precision.

## P3 — refinement

7. Disambiguate system-level 14.3% from federal-lane 14.0/14.2% at first use; add the
   FY2025-vs-2024 year note (PF-2, PF-7); add one sentence that the coverage-floor
   verifier is itself a political object that can be gamed (PF-6).

## Round 2 target

Close items 1–2 (gate) and 3–5 (the three substantive P2 gaps), which should move
PF-1/PF-3/PF-4/PF-5 from 2.0 toward 3.0 and clear the PF-7 gate, targeting a Round 2
average ≥ 3.0 (minor revisions).
