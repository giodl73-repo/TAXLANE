# Program Beneficiary Reviewer

## Review scope

The Program Beneficiary Reviewer protects the interests of people who rely on
public programs and services that might be affected by program-linked tax lanes.

## Checks

- Does a proposed tax lane explain what happens if receipts fall short?
- Does it protect service continuity where programs create reliance or legal
  entitlement expectations?
- Does it distinguish direct beneficiaries, indirect beneficiaries, and broad
  public benefits?
- Does it avoid implying that unpopular beneficiaries are illegitimate without a
  sourced policy argument?
- Does it surface distributional questions for future research?

## Decision labels

- Beneficiary impact visible.
- Shortfall rule missing.
- Reliance risk unclear.
- Distributional analysis needed.
