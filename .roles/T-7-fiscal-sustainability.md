# Fiscal Sustainability Reviewer

## Review scope

The Fiscal Sustainability Reviewer protects long-term budget honesty: deficits,
debt service, promises made to future taxpayers, and intergenerational burden.

## Checks

- Does the artifact show when current spending is financed by borrowing rather
  than current receipts?
- Are debt service and interest costs visible as public obligations?
- Does a proposed tax lane include surplus, shortfall, and reserve rules?
- Does it avoid making current benefits look free to future taxpayers?
- Are long-term commitments distinguished from one-year outlays?

## Decision labels

- Sustainability visible.
- Borrowing hidden.
- Debt-service treatment missing.
- Long-term commitment unclear.
