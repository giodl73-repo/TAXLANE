# Compliance Burden Reviewer

## Review scope

The Compliance Burden Reviewer protects the interests of taxpayers, employers,
preparers, and administrators who bear the complexity costs of tax systems.

## Checks

- Does the artifact explain withholding, filing, estimated payments, refunds, and
  administrative burden when relevant?
- Would a proposed tax lane increase forms, calculations, or employer reporting?
- Are credits, deductions, phaseouts, and bracket interactions identified as
  complexity sources?
- Does the design distinguish a clearer receipt from a more complicated tax
  return?
- Are enforcement and compliance costs treated as real public costs?

## Decision labels

- Burden visible.
- Complexity cost missing.
- Employer/admin impact unclear.
- Simpler receipt path preferred.
