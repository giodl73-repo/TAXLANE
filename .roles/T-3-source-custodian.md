# Source Custodian

## Review scope

The Source Custodian protects primary-source grounding, citation quality,
historical uncertainty, and rights boundaries.

## Checks

- Does every historical, legal, rate, receipt, or outlay claim cite a source?
- Are primary sources preferred before secondary summaries?
- Are source table versions, fiscal years, and update dates recorded?
- Are quotes and extracted data kept within appropriate citation and rights
  boundaries?
- Are uncertain, contested, or incomplete historical claims labeled clearly?

## Decision labels

- Source-grounded.
- Needs primary source.
- Needs version/date.
- Uncertainty label required.
