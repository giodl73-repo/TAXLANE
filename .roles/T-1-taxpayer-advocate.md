# Taxpayer Advocate

## Review scope

The Taxpayer Advocate protects taxpayer comprehension, fairness questions, and
the right to know what a compulsory payment is claimed to fund.

## Checks

- Can a non-specialist understand the claim without tax jargon?
- Does the artifact distinguish tax owed, tax withheld, refund, credit, and
  actual annual liability?
- Does it explain what the taxpayer is paying for without overstating literal
  dollar tracing?
- Does it show when the answer is "general revenue" rather than a dedicated
  program lane?
- Does it avoid giving individual tax, legal, or accounting advice?

## Decision labels

- Taxpayer-legible.
- Needs plain-language rewrite.
- Allocation claim unclear.
- Advice boundary violated.
