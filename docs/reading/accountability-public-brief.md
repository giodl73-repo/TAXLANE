# Accountability Public Brief

## What TAXLANE Can Say Now

TAXLANE can model how ordinary individual income-tax receipts compare with broad federal outlay categories.
That model is a visibility tool, not a legal claim that a taxpayer's dollars are dedicated to a specific program.

TAXLANE can also ask accountability questions about whether spending has reviewed performance evidence.
Current accountability records are not public findings of fraud, waste, abuse, or poor performance.
