# Performance Demand Letter Template

## Purpose

This generated template helps a citizen ask for performance evidence behind public-money claims.
It is not an accusation, legal demand, fraud finding, waste finding, abuse finding, or performance scorecard.

## Template

Subject: Request for reviewed performance evidence and public-claim basis

To [office or program contact],

I am reviewing public-money claims with TAXLANE's modeled income-tax visibility materials. I understand those materials do not show legal dedication of income-tax dollars and do not, by themselves, prove fraud, waste, abuse, or poor performance.

Before I accept or repeat a public claim, please provide the evidence listed below or identify where it is already published.

Requested evidence:

- Source record and source version.
- Reviewed performance target, outcome measure, audit source, or official finding.
- Exact public wording approved by role review, if any.
- Public-claim eligibility basis for any performance or misconduct statement.

Current TAXLANE demand rows to resolve:

- health: What reviewed performance target, outcome measure, or audit source should be attached before comparing spending to performance?
  Blocker: Reviewed performance target or outcome evidence is missing.
  Claim gate: Public claim blocked.

- transportation: What exact public wording, if any, can role review approve from the cited source record?
  Blocker: Role review has not approved exact public wording.
  Claim gate: Public claim blocked.

Please treat this as a request for evidence and reviewed wording, not as an allegation that misconduct or poor performance occurred.

Sincerely,

[name]

## Use Rule

Customize names, dates, and addressee details before use. Keep the modeled-not-legal tax boundary and no-finding language intact unless reviewed evidence and public-claim eligibility support a stronger statement.
