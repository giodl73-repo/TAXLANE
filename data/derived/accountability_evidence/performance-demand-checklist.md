# Performance Demand Checklist

## Purpose

This generated checklist turns TAXLANE accountability blockers into evidence requests a citizen can make before accepting performance or misconduct claims.
It is not a finding of fraud, waste, abuse, or poor performance.

## Before Accepting A Claim

- Ask for the source record and source version.
- Ask for the reviewed performance target, outcome measure, audit source, or official finding.
- Ask whether role review approved the exact public wording.
- Ask whether the record is public-claim eligible.

## Record Checklist

| Lane | Demand This Evidence | Do Not Accept Yet | Claim Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| health | What reviewed performance target, outcome measure, or audit source should be attached before comparing spending to performance? | Reviewed performance target or outcome evidence is missing. | Public claim blocked. |
| transportation | What exact public wording, if any, can role review approve from the cited source record? | Role review has not approved exact public wording. | Public claim blocked. |

## Use Rule

Use this checklist to demand performance evidence and reviewed wording. Do not use it to claim TAXLANE found fraud, waste, abuse, legal dedication of income taxes, or poor performance.
