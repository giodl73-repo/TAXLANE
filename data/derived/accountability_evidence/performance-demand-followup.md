# Performance Demand Follow-Up Template

## Purpose

This generated template helps a citizen follow up when a reply is partial, process-only, or provides no evidence.
It is not an accusation, legal demand, fraud finding, waste finding, abuse finding, or performance scorecard.

## Template

Subject: Follow-up request for missing performance evidence

To [office or program contact],

Thank you for the response. I am treating it as an evidence response, not as proof of misconduct or poor performance.

The reply appears to leave at least one requested item missing or unclear. Please provide the missing item, identify where it is published, or state that the office does not have it.

Missing evidence to clarify:

- Source record and source version, if not already provided.
- Reviewed performance target, outcome measure, audit source, or official finding, if not already provided.
- Exact role-approved public wording, if any.
- Public-claim eligibility basis for any performance or misconduct statement.

Current unresolved TAXLANE demand rows:

- health: Reviewed performance target or outcome evidence is missing.
  Follow-up ask: What reviewed performance target, outcome measure, or audit source should be attached before comparing spending to performance?
  Claim gate remains: Public claim blocked.

- transportation: Role review has not approved exact public wording.
  Follow-up ask: What exact public wording, if any, can role review approve from the cited source record?
  Claim gate remains: Public claim blocked.

Please keep this as an evidence clarification request. If the evidence does not exist or is not yet reviewed, a clear statement of that status is useful.

Sincerely,

[name]

## Use Rule

Use this follow-up only after a reply leaves requested evidence missing or unclear. Do not use a missing or incomplete reply to claim TAXLANE found fraud, waste, abuse, legal dedication of income taxes, poor performance, or proven reform benefits.
