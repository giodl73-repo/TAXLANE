# Public Accountability Questions

## Purpose

These generated questions are safe to ask publicly because they request reviewed evidence or role-approved wording.
They are not findings of fraud, waste, abuse, or poor performance.

## Questions

| Lane | Public-Safe Question | Why This Is Still Blocked |
|---|---|---|
| health | What reviewed performance target, outcome measure, or audit source should be attached before comparing spending to performance? | Reviewed performance target or outcome evidence is missing. |
| transportation | What exact public wording, if any, can role review approve from the cited source record? | Role review has not approved exact public wording. |

## Use Rule

Use these questions to ask for evidence. Do not present the underlying draft records as public claims.
