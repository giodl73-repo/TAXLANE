# Accountability Evidence Readiness Report

## Purpose

This report classifies draft accountability evidence records by public-claim readiness.
It is not a list of fraud, waste, abuse, or performance findings.

## Readiness States

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `EvidenceOnly` | Internal evidence review only; not ready for public claims. |
| `NeedsRoleReview` | Source/accountability reviewed and waiting for public wording review. |
| `PublicClaimEligible` | Role reviewed with official finding or adjudicated status. |
