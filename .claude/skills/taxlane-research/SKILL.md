# TAXLANE Research

Use this skill before standardizing a tax-system explanation, making a historical
claim, comparing reform designs, or preparing public-facing reading packets.

## Workflow

1. State the research question and the decision it informs.
2. Inspect local evidence first: README, product plan, wave docs, research notes,
   and dependency trackers.
3. Check external primary sources when making historical, legal, fiscal, or
   budget-process claims.
4. Record findings with IDs, citations, implications, confidence, and non-goals.
5. Split recommendations into adopt now, prototype, and defer/reject.
6. Run or cite validation commands for measurable claims.

## Output

Write a repo-local research note under `docs/research/`, then update affected
wave docs, public reading packets, or TRACKER dependency records.
