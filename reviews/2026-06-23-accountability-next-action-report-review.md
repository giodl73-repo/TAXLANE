# Accountability Next-Action Report Review

## Scope

This review covers the generated next-action column in the accountability
evidence readiness report.

## Decision

Accept the next-action column as internal operational guidance.

The next actions are deliberately limited to review steps: complete role review,
attach reviewed performance targets or outcome evidence, prepare exact public
wording after eligibility, or continue evidence review. They are not scores,
findings, or public accusations.

## Findings

| Role | Result |
|---|---|
| Reform Skeptic | Pass: next actions do not infer fraud, waste, abuse, or poor performance. |
| Source Custodian | Pass: missing-evidence records direct researchers to attach reviewed evidence. |
| Taxpayer Advocate | Pass: the report makes accountability work actionable without overclaiming. |
| Maintainer | Pass: report validation checks generated next actions for staleness. |

## Guardrails

- Do not display next actions as public findings.
- Do not treat missing performance evidence as proof of nonperformance.
- Keep exact public wording gated by role review.
