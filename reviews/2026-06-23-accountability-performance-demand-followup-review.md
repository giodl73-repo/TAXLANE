# Accountability Performance Demand Follow-Up Review

## Scope

Reviewed `performance-demand-followup.md`, its Rust generator, and VTRACE rows.

## Findings

- The template asks for missing source, performance, wording, and public-claim
  basis evidence after an incomplete reply.
- The template says the reply is treated as an evidence response, not proof of
  misconduct or poor performance.
- The row-specific follow-ups preserve current blockers and keep claim gates
  blocked.
- Rust validation checks generated Markdown for staleness and requires README
  discoverability.

## Decision

Accepted as a public-safe follow-up template. It helps readers narrow missing
evidence without escalating gaps into accusations or findings.
