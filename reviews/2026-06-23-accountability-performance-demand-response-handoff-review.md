# Accountability Performance Demand Response Handoff Review

## Scope

Reviewed the generated response handoff and validation gate for response
tracking artifacts.

## Findings

- The handoff routes readers to the response dashboard, log, rubric, and
  follow-up template by task.
- The handoff routes implementers to JSONL, schema, and status artifacts without
  requiring Markdown scraping.
- Current counts remain 2 response rows, 2 not-yet-received rows, 0 allowed
  public claims, and 2 blocked public claims.
- The use rule preserves the no-finding boundary for fraud, waste, abuse, legal
  dedication, poor performance, and reform benefits.

## Decision

Accepted as a task-first response tracking handoff. It improves navigation for
citizen readers and product implementers without changing evidence records or
public-claim eligibility.
